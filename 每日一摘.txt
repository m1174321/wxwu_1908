We all carry something with us.
我们都在负重前行。

哭能让我停下脚步，去感受生活中碰到的挫折与困难。

You can never replace anyone. What is lost is lost.
每个人都是无可替代的，失去了便是失去了。

We don't get a chance to do that many things, and every one should be really excellent.
我们这一生能做的事情有限，所以要把每件事都做到完美。

Everyone's the hero in their own story.
每个人都是自己故事里的英雄。

Tough time don't last, tough people do.
没有过不去的坎, 只有打不倒的人。

A real man makes his own luck.
有能力的人自己创造运气。

It is only with the heart that one can see rightly. What is essential is invisible to the eye.

Nothing is necessary. Everything is a choice.
没有什么事是必然的，每件事都只是一个选择的问题。

I hope you never lack the courage to start over again.
希望你任何时候都不缺从头再来的勇气。

If you're not satisfied with the life you're living, don't just complain. Do something about it.
对于现况的不满，你不能只是抱怨，而是要有勇气作出改变。
